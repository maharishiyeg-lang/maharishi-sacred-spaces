The Origin

Eric Swanson is the visionary founder of Maharishi Sacred Spaces — a living, evolving sanctuary dedicated to the intersection of quantum science and ancient spiritual wisdom. His journey began not in a classroom or a temple, but in the quiet depths of personal transformation, where the questions that could not be answered by ordinary means led him into the extraordinary.

Drawn to the mysteries of consciousness from an early age, Eric began exploring the invisible architecture of the human experience — the energetic fields, the sacred patterns, and the quantum principles that underlie all of existence. What he discovered was not a single tradition, but a convergence: a place where chakra wisdom, sacred geometry, and the science of the quantum field all pointed toward the same truth.

The Mission

The mission of Maharishi Sacred Spaces is both simple and profound: to aid others in their alignment with self, using sacred tools and techniques to create balance. Eric believes that every human being carries within them a sacred space — a still point of infinite intelligence — and that the work of spiritual practice is simply the art of returning to that space, again and again, until it becomes home.

Through his growing community on Instagram and Facebook, Eric has shared over 250 pieces of sacred content — from immersive Chakra Series deep-dives to sacred tool explorations, from consciousness teachings to energetic activations. Each post is a breadcrumb on the path; each teaching, an invitation to go deeper.

The Work Today

Today, Eric channels his decades of study and practice into two landmark offerings: his debut Maharishi Sacred Spaces Workbook — a transformative guide available as a digital download, with a hardcover edition forthcoming — and The Power Pyramid: The Power of 3, a guided meditation experience rooted in sacred geometry and the mystical law of three.

These offerings are not products. They are portals — carefully crafted gateways into the sacred space that lives within you. Eric's work is an invitation: to slow down, to go inward, and to remember what you have always known.
