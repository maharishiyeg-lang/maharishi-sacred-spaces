Eric Swanson

Quantum Spiritual Guide  ·  Author  ·  Teacher

Eric Swanson is the founder of Maharishi Sacred Spaces — a sanctuary of consciousness, sacred tools, and transformative practice. Drawing from the deep wellspring of quantum spirituality, Eric has devoted his life to guiding others toward alignment with their truest self.

His work bridges the ancient and the quantum: weaving together chakra wisdom, sacred geometry, and the science of consciousness into a living, breathing practice. Through his teachings, Eric illuminates the hidden architecture of the human energy field — helping seekers unlock their personal power, speak their truth, and awaken to the divine intelligence that lives within.

With over 251 posts of sacred content shared across his community, Eric has cultivated a growing circle of souls drawn to the mysteries of energy, light, and inner transformation. His Chakra Series, Membership Tiers, and sacred tool offerings reflect a commitment to making spiritual wisdom accessible, experiential, and deeply personal.

Now, Eric brings his teachings into their most potent form yet — a debut book and a guided meditation experience designed to initiate a new chapter in your spiritual journey.
